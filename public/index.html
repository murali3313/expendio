Expendio Privacy Policy

Manage all your expenses in one place.

Privacy

  • This application doesn't need the internet connection for it to function. Which means that it strictly doesn't send any of your information outside.
  • BUT, when you use voice-enabled functionality, it will send your voice to google server to convert it to text. This is not done using any third party libraries, but only internal to what GOOGLE provides via the Android framework.
  • When you use Google cloud sync then your data will be stored in YOUR drive storage with Google, So that app will backup your data in cloud so that your information is not lost, when you change device or if any crash happens
  • For sharing data across family members we will not be using any host server to mediate the data transfers, but instead, we will use basic Bluetooth, SMS based communication. So no worries of loss/ theft of data of any kind.

Audio Capability

  • Voice enabled expense input for the easy and quick expense addition
  • Support of colloquial way of telling days like today, yesterday, tomorrow
  • Support for voices which has the date like 2nd September, October 28th will be processed to appropriate expenses.

Tagging

  • Capability to tag your expenses.
  • Capability to create or modify tags and add appropriate words which can define your tag
  • Capability to visualize your expenses tag-wise in a pie chart
  • Capability to compare your expenses with previous months using a grouped bar chart.

Recurring Expenses

  • Capability to define your recurring expenses, which could be daily, or on a particular day of the week or on a specific day of the month
  • Expendio will create appropriate expenses and will intimate you in the time you want and you can review it and accept or discard accordingly.

Excel generation

  • Reports of your monthly expenses can be generated month wise or for all months in one go.
  • You can make the copy of your expenses and store it in your preferred storage drives one eg, is Google drive.
  • Reports generated has the formula, so even after extracting it, if you add any expense to the excel it will re-compute your expenses.

SMS Expense

  • Capability to suggest expense based on your expense from bank or any other way
  • You can program Expendio, with certain phrases that will be in the smses to make Expendio to uniquely identify the sms and suggest you with expenses
